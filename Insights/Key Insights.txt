Key Insights

Technology is the highest-performing product category, contributing the largest share of total sales and profit.
Office Supplies generated consistent sales, while Furniture recorded comparatively lower profitability.
The business achieved **Total Sales of approximately $2.30 Million, reflecting strong overall revenue generation.
Total Profit reached approximately $286K, indicating healthy business performance despite varying profit margins across product categories.
The West region contributed the highest percentage of sales (31.6%), followed by the **East** (29.5%), making them the strongest regional markets.
The Central and South regions contributed a smaller share of sales, highlighting opportunities for market expansion and targeted business strategies.
Sales fluctuated over time, indicating the presence of seasonal demand patterns and changing customer purchasing behavior.
Interactive Region and Category filters enable users to analyze performance across different business segments and identify high-performing or underperforming areas.
The dashboard provides decision-makers with a clear overview of sales trends, regional performance, category-wise profitability, and overall business growth.
These insights can help businesses optimize inventory, improve marketing strategies, focus on profitable product categories, and support data-driven decision-making.
